Hi, I'm Gunjan Khut,

A Robotics Controls Engineer with a passion for innovation and a drive to push the boundaries of technology, I thrive on creating solutions that merge cutting-edge hardware with intelligent software. With a strong academic foundation from the University of Maryland, College Park, and 11+ years of professional experience in Robotics, Industrial Automation, and Controls, I have honed my expertise in a variety of areas.

Throughout my career, I have been at the forefront of designing and implementing state-of-the-art robotics projects, harnessing the power of computer vision, AI planning, and advanced control algorithms. My proficiency in industrial automation, computer vision, machine learning, deep learning, Data Science and AI planning has led me to drive IoT, Industry 4.0 initiatives and sustainable manufacturing practices. Notably, I innovated a data-science tool for calculating and analyzing solar power plants.

My diverse skill set includes programming software's, applying my expertise in Robot Control, Kinematics, and Dynamics. I possess a keen eye for Computer Vision and Machine Vision applications and excel in Mechatronics and Electromechanical design. My experience with Embedded Systems has enabled me to create robust and efficient solutions.

Having worked in a 80K Sqft. Cleanroom manufacturing facility, I have extensive experience in High speed manufacturing, rapid prototyping, laboratory testing, and simulation, ensuring that each project meets the highest standards of quality and performance. My proficiency in 3D CAD modeling and generating manufacturing drawings and control schematics enables seamless product development.

I actively collaborate and contribute to open-source software projects, fostering a spirit of knowledge sharing and community growth. My expertise extends to Linear & Optimal Control, Robot Localization & Navigation, Sensor Integration and Fusion.

I have had the opportunity to work with a wide array of software and hardware technologies, including Git, Linux, MATLAB, OpenCV, and SolidWorks. My hardware expertise encompasses Industrial Robots (ABB, Fanuc & UR), Keyence/Cognex Vision Systems, SuperTrak, BnR Controllers, Mobile Platforms, RGBD sensors, Micro-controllers, AC & DC motors, PLC, HMI & VFDs, Linear Stages, and Pneumatic Actuators.

My ultimate goal is to make robotics accessible to all, empowering individuals and industries to achieve remarkable feats through advanced technology. If you are interested in connecting for professional purposes or discussing potential collaborations, feel free to reach out to me
